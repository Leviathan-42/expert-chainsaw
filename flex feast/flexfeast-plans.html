Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or change your delivery frequency anytime before your next delivery. Changes take effect on your next scheduled delivery.

What if I don't like a meal?

No problem! You can swap out any meal for something else from our rotating menu. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee - if you're not happy, we'll make it right.

Are there any hidden fees?

Nope! The price you see is what you pay. No delivery fees, no service charges, no surprises. Just honest, transparent pricing.

How long do meals stay fresh?

Our meals stay fresh in your fridge for 5-7 days from delivery. Each meal is labeled with a "best by" date for optimal freshness and flavor.

Can I skip weeks or pause my subscription?

Yes! Skip individual weeks or pause your subscription entirely. There's no commitment and no fees for pausing. Just resume when you're ready.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

We offer vegetarian, gluten-free, and dairy-free options clearly marked in our menu. Our customization tools make it easy to find meals that fit your dietary needs.
